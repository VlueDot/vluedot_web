What is Veep?

It is an advanced communication and resource management solution built on top of VCore. It transforms traditional messaging into a unified, intelligent ecosystem by seamlessly integrating multiple communication channels with a suite of productivity tools and AI-powered capabilities. At its core, Veep leverages AI to automate and enhance everyday interactions. It provides customizable, topic-specific chat environments that help maintain privacy and security by isolating sensitive information.
